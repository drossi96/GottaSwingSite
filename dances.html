Our Dances

Gottaswing brings you the best in weekly, monthly, and special event dances with recorded and live music featuring local, national, and internationally recognized swing bands. Dancers of all ages, all styles, and all levels of experience are welcome. You never need a partner and dances include a beginner swing lesson in the price of admission. In addition to regularly-scheduled weekly dances, we also have great special events you won't want to miss. Scroll down this list of regular dances, but don't forget to check our CALENDAR frequently for special events.

Saturday Night Dances at Glen Echo Park

Gottaswing is delighted to host swing dances at the beautiful, historic Spanish Ballroom and the Bumper Car Pavilion at Glen Echo Park. Admission $20/person advanced sales (credit card only); $30/person at the door. Advanced ticket sales end at 12noon the day of the dance. Don't miss the fun!

Details and tickets HERE.

Friday Night Dances at Colvin Run Dance Hall

Join Sue Caley for our popular Friday night dances at Colvin Run Dance Hall with twice-monthly dances on the first and third Friday of the month. Beginner swing lesson 8-8:30pm, dance 8:30-11pm, $20/advance, $25/door. Advanced ticket sales end at 5pm the day of the dance.
